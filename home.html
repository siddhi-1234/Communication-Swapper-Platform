About SwapX

SwapX is a community-driven platform where learning meets sharing. Whether you want to learn a new language, coding, music, or fitness, you can connect with others who have the skills you need—and share what you know in return.

  • ✅ Exchange skills without spending money
  • ✅ Build meaningful connections and learn together
  • ✅ Grow your expertise and help others grow theirs
